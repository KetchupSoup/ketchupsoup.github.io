History and Design of the Electric Guitar

The electric guitar is one of the most iconic, if not the single most important instrument of the 20th century. It is an instrument that just about everyone has heard in some way, shape or form. It has defined entire genres through its versatility, capable of everything from from a soft hum to a full tilt screech. In recent years, the electric guitar can even be used as a synth controller!
